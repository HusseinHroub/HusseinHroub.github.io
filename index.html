About the App

Score Keeper: Game Tracker

Score Keeper is a simple and fast score tracking app for card games, board games, and any multiplayer game where you need to keep score. Perfect for card games like Trix, Baloot, Tarneeb, Rummy, Basra, and more — no paper, no pen, no hassle.

👥
2–5 Teams or Players
Supports up to 5 teams in a single game — great for large groups and big game nights.
📊
Running Totals
The app automatically calculates and displays the running total after every round.
✏️
Edit Anytime
Add, edit, or delete score entries at any point during the game without losing progress.
📁
Full Game History
Every game is automatically saved. Browse past games, search by team name, and reload any session.
🔍
Search Games
Quickly find any past game by searching through your history by team or player name.
✨
Clean Interface
Simple, distraction-free design — no clutter, no learning curve, just tap and score.
